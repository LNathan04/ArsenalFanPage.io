  • Nico Williams Several elite European teams, notably Arsenal, have expressed interest in Athletic Bilbao's gifted winger Nico Williams especially since the Euros. Williams, who is well-known for his quick speed and dribbling abilities, might provide Arsenal a new offensive option on the wings.
